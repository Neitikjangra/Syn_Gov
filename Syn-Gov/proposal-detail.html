Full Proposal

We propose allocating ₹20,000 from the club treasury to organize our annual hackathon event. This has been our flagship event for the past three years and continues to grow in scale and impact.

Budget Breakdown:

  • Venue rental: ₹5,000
  • Food and beverages (2 days): ₹6,000
  • Prizes (1st, 2nd, 3rd): ₹5,000
  • Branding and marketing materials: ₹2,500
  • Miscellaneous and contingency: ₹1,500

The event will be a 36-hour hackathon open to all college students. We plan to invite mentors from local tech companies and alumni who have previously participated. Last year's event saw 120 participants — we expect 150+ this year.

All spending will be documented and shared with club members within 7 days of the event's conclusion. Receipts will be uploaded to our shared drive for full transparency.
